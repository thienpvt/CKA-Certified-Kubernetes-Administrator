
4. Live cluster confirms: PVC binds in ~8m59s. PV reports `Bound` to `cka-sim-storage-01/app-data`.

**Interpretation:**

Kubernetes PV/PVC binding controller matches on `capacity`, `accessModes`, `storageClassName`, `volumeMode`, and `selector` — **not** `nodeAffinity`. A hostPath PV without `nodeAffinity` will bind to any matching PVC immediately. The `nodeAffinity` constraint only fires later at **Pod scheduling time**, when the scheduler attempts to place a Pod that mounts the PVC.

Author originally designed the trap thinking it would keep the PVC `Pending`. Grader was rewritten in Phase 07.1 D-25 once the binding behavior was understood (PVC-bound check reduced to `weight=0` informational), but the question text and the setup.sh comment still describe the obsolete (and incorrect) mental model.

<objective>
Author Q02 `02-netpol-dns-egress` — a two-stage NetworkPolicy troubleshooting question per RESEARCH §4 "Q2 netpol — exact subtle misconfig". Tracker coverage: Troubleshoot NetworkPolicy (PACK-07 primary).

The broken state is intentionally subtle:
- A default-deny-all-egress NetworkPolicy is active in the lab ns.
- A second NetworkPolicy `allow-web-to-api` is meant to grant egress from pods labelled `app=web` to the `api-svc` backend on TCP/8080. Its `podSelector.matchLabels` is `app: web` but the actual Deployment pods use `app.kubernetes.io/name: web` — so the allow policy selects no pods.
- The DNS allow rule is missing entirely. Even after the label-drift fix, DNS resolution still fails until the candidate adds UDP/53 to kube-system.

Two-stage fix: (1) correct the selector key to match real pod labels; (2) add a DNS-allow egress rule.

Output: 6 question files + 3 fixtures. 8 minutes estimated (per RESEARCH §1).
</objective>
